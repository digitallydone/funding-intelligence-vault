## Objection Handling

**"I already have a spreadsheet for this."**
The Vault isn't a template to fill in — it's a workspace already populated with 75+ funders. The value is the research, not the structure.

**"Isn't this information available online for free?"**
Yes — over dozens of websites, inconsistently organized, with no fit assessment or next-action guidance. The Vault saves the hours of aggregation and assessment that's already been done.

**"How current is the data?"**
The dataset reflects current program information as of launch. Funding programs change, and customers are encouraged to verify details on funder websites. Think of it as an intelligence starting point, not a live database.

**"$49 seems high for a spreadsheet."**
This isn't a spreadsheet — it's a pre-researched funding intelligence system. If you bill $50/hour for grant consulting, the Vault pays for itself in the first hour it saves you. For an in-house team, the savings are even greater.
