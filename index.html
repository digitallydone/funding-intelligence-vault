FAQ

Common questions

Is this a spreadsheet I have to fill in myself?
No — and it's not a spreadsheet at all. The primary delivery is a web app (built on Softr) where you log in, search, filter, and track funders directly in your browser. A Google Sheets copy-template and CSV are also included as backup formats. Nothing to fill in. Everything is already researched and organized.
What funding opportunities are included?
The current dataset focuses on Canadian funding opportunities across federal programs, provincial grants, and private foundations. Sectors include arts & culture, community development, environment, health, youth, education, Indigenous programming, and social enterprise. The data includes deadline types, amounts, eligibility, and fit scores.
What is the Airtable white-glove setup?
If you'd prefer to work in Airtable rather than Google Sheets, we'll configure your Airtable base with the same data and structure, set up views for filtering and sorting, and deliver it ready to use. This is an optional add-on — just mention it when you contact us after purchase.
What does "early access" mean?
Early access customers get in at the lowest price before the product launches publicly. You also get priority access to future dataset updates, expanded sectors, and new features as the Vault grows.
How do I get access after I pay?
You'll be redirected to a confirmation page immediately after payment. The Google Sheets link and onboarding instructions are delivered there and by email within a few hours of your purchase.
Is this useful if I'm not in Canada?
The current dataset is focused on Canadian funders. If you're a Canadian-based organization or working with Canadian clients, this is designed specifically for you. Future editions may expand to other geographies.
